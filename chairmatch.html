The Chairs match

The chairs match is a bit simpler to explain. To win, you must pin you opponent or make them submit. However, the use of chairs is legal and very encouraged.

The rules are very simple and straightforward. This type is using used to add more tension to a low-profile match. While it may seem uninteresting, but this opens many chances for imagination. Many wrestlers will incorporate the chair into their usual moves to make them more painful.

Some popular chairs matches include Batista vs the Undertaker and John Cena vs Wade Barrett.
